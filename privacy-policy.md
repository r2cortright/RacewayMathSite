# Raceway Math Privacy Policy

_Last updated: November 2025_

Thank you for using **Raceway Math**, an educational app developed by **Solstice 1**.

This page describes how the app handles information and what data, if any, is collected when you use it.

---

## Information Collection

Raceway Math does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal information.  
The app runs entirely on your device and does not connect to external servers.

- No login or registration is required.  
- No analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs are included.  
- No user-generated content is transmitted or stored.  
- All gameplay data (such as scores or progress) remains local to the device.  

---

## Children’s Privacy

Raceway Math is designed for children and families.  
Because no personal data is collected or transmitted, it fully complies with COPPA (Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act) requirements.

---

## In-App Purchases and Advertising

- **No in-app purchases.**  
- **No third-party ads.**  
- The app experience is free of external links or content that would lead users outside the app environment.

---

## Internet Access

Raceway Math does not require an internet connection for gameplay.  
The only time the app may open an external link is when a user voluntarily taps on a link to:
- View this privacy policy, or  
- Visit the official Solstice 1 website or App Store page.  

These links open in the user’s default browser.
